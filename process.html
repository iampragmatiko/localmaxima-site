PROCESS

HOW THIS
WORKS

Local Maxima is not a portfolio archive. It is a workshop loop where Nous cron runs surface ideas, Andrea selects what deserves attention, and the useful ones become living prototype pages.

01 · CRON

Ideas arrive

Scheduled Nous runs generate candidate concepts, constraints, and prototype prompts.

02 · PAGE

Artifacts publish

Approved ideas become living pages only after there is a real demo, packet, script, or receipt.

03 · FEEDBACK

Andrea edits

Feedback is captured as a specific revision target, not vibes dissolving into chat steam.

04 · ITERATE

The page learns

Each useful correction becomes a dated update. Dead branches get archived instead of worshipped.

PUBLIC WORKSHOP CONTRACT

Cron can suggest. Pages must prove.

Cron jobs generate candidate ideas on a schedule, but the public site only gets built artifacts and honest process surfaces. Candidate ideas stay private until Andrea approves a real page treatment.

PAGE REQUIRES

problem, mechanism, artifact, status, next feedback needed, guardrails, and an iteration log.

FEEDBACK WORKS TODAY

Andrea can leave a local note on a living page, paste it into chat, and Nous turns it into a dated revision.

AUTHORITY LIMIT

Prototype pages can store preferences, drafts, and receipts. They cannot imply purchase, payment, or dangerous automation authority.

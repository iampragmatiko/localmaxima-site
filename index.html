PUBLIC WORKSHOP CONTRACT

Cron can suggest. Pages must prove.

Cron jobs generate candidate ideas on a schedule, but the public site only gets two kinds of surfaces: built artifacts and explicit process scaffolding. Candidate ideas stay private until Andrea approves a real page treatment.

PAGE REQUIRES

problem, mechanism, artifact, status, next feedback needed, guardrails, and an iteration log.

FEEDBACK WORKS TODAY

Andrea can leave a local note on a living page, paste it into chat, and Nous turns it into a dated revision.

AUTHORITY LIMIT

Prototype pages can store preferences, drafts, and receipts. They cannot imply purchase, payment, or dangerous automation authority.
